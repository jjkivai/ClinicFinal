Who We Are

Karen Cardiocare Clinic is a private Healthcare facility where We Diagnose and Treat heart and other medical conditions. We educate on health issues, screen for diseases, and perform total body wellness checkups.

Our Mission
  • To provide affordable diagnosis and treatment
  • To educate our patients to improve health
  • To partner with other institutions for patient refferals as needed
Our Vision
  • To be a Regional Center for Health Solutions
  • Reduce the burden of Non-Communicable diseases (NCD)
  • Reduce the burden of NCD complications
Our Values
  • Care: We give the best and walk the health journey with our Patients
  • Empathy: We give personalised support in diagnosis and treatment
  • Respect: We respect our Patients' choice of treatment
